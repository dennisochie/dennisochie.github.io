SIAYA TOWNSHIP PRIMARY SCHOOL


Siaya Township Primary School is an ordinary public educational institute in Siaya Township, Karemo, Kenya.

This school is managed / sponsored by central government and offers Kenya Certificate of Primary Education (KCPE).
The Kenya Certificate of Primary Education which is controlled by the Kenya National Examination Council (KNEC) under Ministry of Education.
